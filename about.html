Our Story

Welcome to Bean & Leaf Cafe. Founded in 2018, we started with a simple mission: to bring the finest ethically sourced coffee and hand-picked teas to our local community. What began as a small family espresso cart has grown into a cozy neighborhood gathering space.

We believe that a great cup of tea or coffee is more than just a drink—it is a moment of peace in a busy day.

What We Stand For

  • Locally Roasted Beans: All our coffee beans are roasted in small batches right here in town to ensure maximum freshness.
  • Global Loose-Leaf Teas: We source our organic teas directly from sustainable, fair-trade farms across the globe.
  • Community Focus: We proudly support local bakers by featuring their fresh pastries in our shop every morning.

Meet the Founders

Nihal Pieris

Nihal is a certified tea sommelier with a passion for herbal blends, he has spent over a decade perfecting his craft as a master barista. He created Bean & Leaf to share their love for quality brews with you.
